is nonideological, nonpolitical and religiously uncommitted.
Our associtions is aiming to
      • prevent the illnesses and accidents of skeleton in people, caused by their
        special size,

      • contribute the availability of anatomically healthy footwears and ergonomically
        right designed products for short and tall, small- and biglegged people,

      • make connections between manufacturer, traders and our association,

      • make connections among members,

      • prevent the standing down caused by the special size,

      • support the members to develop their positive selfimage,

      • spread general knowledge of the problems caused by special size
        and revoke them,

      • achieve equality between special sized people and other finnish
        consumers,

      • co-operate with other similar international associations.
