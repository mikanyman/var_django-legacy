          About the association's activities in 2004-2005
          to promote equality in Finland and our partners

          **********


          Our shortness or tallness and the size of out feet are a feature,
          which we are not able to influence on even by changing our ways of living.
          Because of our height we pay in certain cases more taxes than other citizens do.
          For more information read the letter that we sent to Mr Osmo Soinivaara,
          Minister of Health and Social Services, and Mr Gunnar Jansson, a Member of
          Finland.s Parliament and Vice-Chairman of the draft committee
          of the EU charter at the time when letter was sent.
